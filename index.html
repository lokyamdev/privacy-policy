Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Calorie Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Body Weight Data

The app allows users to record and manage body weight information. All weight records are stored locally on the user's device. We do not store this information on our servers.

b) Analytics Information

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data. This may include:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App interactions
  • General usage statistics

This information helps us improve app performance and user experience.

c) Crash Reports

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash logs and diagnostic information. This data helps us detect, analyze, and fix technical issues.

d) Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers and other information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

2. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. However, certain data may be processed by third-party service providers including:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob

These services operate under their own privacy policies. You can review Google's Privacy Policy here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Data Storage and Security

Body weight records are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a remote database of your personal health information.

Third-party services such as Firebase and AdMob implement their own security measures to protect collected data.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Your Choices

You may:

  • Disable personalized ads through your device settings.
  • Stop using the app to prevent further data collection.
  • Uninstall the app at any time.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
