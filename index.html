Privacy Policy

Calorie Calculator

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Calorie Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Body weight data. The app allows you to enter and manage body weight information. These records are stored locally on your device. We do not store body weight records on our servers.

Account information. If you choose to sign in, we collect your Google account email address and a unique user identifier provided by Google. This information is stored securely on our backend (Supabase) and is used to authenticate you, link your subscription, and enable account-dependent features such as the AI Coach.

Subscription and entitlement data. If you purchase a subscription through Google Play, we receive a subscription token and related metadata from Google (product ID, purchase token, subscription status) to verify and manage your entitlements. Payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play; we do not receive or store your payment card details.

AI Coach messages. If you use the AI Coach feature, the messages you send are transmitted to our backend and processed by OpenAI to generate a response. Your conversation history is stored locally on your device. We do not permanently retain your AI Coach messages on our servers beyond what is required to generate each response.

Health Connect data. If you choose to enable Health Connect integration and grant permission, the app may read body weight data from Health Connect and save imported records locally on your device for use within the app.

Analytics information. We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect usage data such as device type and model, operating system version, app interactions, and general usage statistics.

Crash reports. We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash logs and diagnostic information to detect, analyze, and fix technical issues.

Advertising data. We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to users on free plans. AdMob may collect device identifiers and related information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, subject to applicable settings and consent requirements. Subscribers on paid plans do not see ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide calorie, profile, and weight-tracking features.
  • Authenticate your account and manage your subscription entitlements.
  • Power the AI Coach by sending your messages and weight context to OpenAI.
  • Improve app stability, performance, and usability.
  • Monitor crashes and diagnose errors.
  • Display ads to users on free plans.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Some information may be processed by third-party service providers that support app functionality:

  • Supabase — backend authentication and entitlement storage.
  • OpenAI — processes AI Coach messages to generate responses. Messages you send to the AI Coach are subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing — handles subscription purchases and provides subscription status to our backend.
  • Google Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics — usage analytics and crash reporting.
  • Google AdMob — advertisement delivery for free-plan users.

If you enable Health Connect integration, the app accesses body weight data from Health Connect only with your explicit permission.

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies. You can review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy and Supabase's Privacy Policy at https://supabase.com/privacy.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Locally stored app data. Body weight records, profile settings, app preferences, AI Coach conversation history, and locally imported Health Connect copies remain on your device until you delete them.

You can delete all locally stored app data at any time from within the app by opening Settings and selecting Delete all local data. You can also remove local data by clearing the app's storage from your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Because we do not store your body weight or AI Coach conversation history on our own servers, we cannot delete that data remotely for you.

Account data. If you sign in, your account information (email and user ID) is stored on our backend. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email address listed below.

Health Connect data. If you granted access to Health Connect, you can manage or delete your Health Connect source data through Health Connect settings on your device. Deleting local app data from Calorie Calculator does not remove the original data stored in Health Connect.

Third-party service data. Analytics, crash, and advertising data collected by Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and AdMob may be retained by Google according to Google's own policies and retention practices. OpenAI's retention of AI Coach messages is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy. We do not directly control those retention periods.

5. Data Storage and Security

Body weight records and AI Coach conversation history are stored locally on your device. Account and subscription information is stored on Supabase-hosted infrastructure. We do not maintain a remote database of your body weight or Health Connect data.

Communication between the app and our backend uses HTTPS. Third-party services such as Supabase, Firebase, and AdMob implement their own security measures to protect collected data.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

  • Sign out of your account at any time from within the app under Profile > Sign out.
  • Request deletion of your account data by contacting us at the email below.
  • Decline or revoke Health Connect permissions through your device settings.
  • Disable personalized ads through your device settings, where available.
  • Use the in-app Delete all local data option to erase locally stored data.
  • Stop using the app to prevent further collection of analytics, crash, or advertising-related data.
  • Uninstall the app at any time.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
