Privacy Policy

Calorie Calculator

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Calorie Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Body weight data. The app allows you to enter and manage body weight information. These records are stored locally on your device. We do not store body weight records on our servers.

Health Connect data. If you choose to enable Health Connect integration and grant permission, the app may read body weight data from Health Connect and save imported records locally on your device for use within the app.

Analytics information. We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect usage data such as device type and model, operating system version, app interactions, and general usage statistics.

Crash reports. We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash logs and diagnostic information to detect, analyze, and fix technical issues.

Advertising data. We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and related information to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, subject to applicable settings and consent requirements.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide calorie, profile, and weight-tracking features.
  • Improve app stability, performance, and usability.
  • Monitor crashes and diagnose errors.
  • Display ads within the app.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Some information may be processed by third-party service providers that support app functionality, including Google Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google AdMob.

If you enable Health Connect integration, the app accesses body weight data from Health Connect only with your permission.

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies. You can review Google's Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Locally stored app data. Body weight records, profile settings, app preferences, and locally imported Health Connect copies remain on your device until you delete them.

You can delete all locally stored app data at any time from within the app by opening Settings and selecting Delete all local data. You can also remove local data by clearing the app's storage from your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Because we do not store this local data on our own servers, we cannot delete it remotely for you.

Health Connect data. If you granted access to Health Connect, you can manage or delete your Health Connect source data through Health Connect settings on your device. Deleting local app data from Calorie Calculator does not remove the original data stored in Health Connect.

Third-party service data. Analytics, crash, and advertising data collected by Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and AdMob may be retained by Google according to Google's own policies and retention practices. We do not directly control those retention periods.

5. Data Storage and Security

Body weight records used by the app are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain a remote database of your body weight or Health Connect data.

Third-party services such as Firebase and AdMob implement their own security measures to protect collected data.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

  • Decline or revoke Health Connect permissions through your device settings.
  • Disable personalized ads through your device settings, where available.
  • Use the in-app Delete all local data option to erase local app data.
  • Stop using the app to prevent further collection of analytics, crash, or advertising-related data.
  • Uninstall the app at any time.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
